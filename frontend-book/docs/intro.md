# Introduction to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the educational module on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This module is designed for AI students and developers transitioning into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## About This Module

This educational module covers the complete stack for Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, from foundational concepts to advanced integration. The module covers:

- **Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System** - Introduction to ROS 2 fundamentals, communication primitives, and robot modeling with URDF.
- **Module 2: The Digital Twin** - Digital twin concepts, physics simulation with Gazebo, and high-fidelity interaction with Unity.
- **Module 3: The AI-Robot Brain** - Perception, localization, and navigation for humanoid robots using NVIDIA Isaac.
- **Module 4: Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** - Integrating vision, language models, and robotic actions to enable natural humanoid interaction.

## Target Audience

This module is designed for:
- AI students looking to understand robotics
- Developers transitioning from software development to robotics
- Anyone interested in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics

## Learning Outcomes

By completing this module, you will:
- Understand what ROS 2 is and why it is critical for humanoid robots
- Learn about ROS 2 architecture and DDS-based communication
- Understand the role of ROS 2 in real-world vs simulated robots
- Master ROS 2 communication primitives: Nodes, Topics, Services, and Actions
- Learn how to connect Python AI agents to robot controllers using rclpy
- Understand robot modeling with URDF in humanoid robotics
- Know how URDF enables simulation, control, and visualization
- Learn about digital twin concepts in humanoid robotics
- Understand physics simulation with Gazebo
- Master high-fidelity interaction with Unity
- Understand NVIDIA Isaac for perception, localization, and navigation in humanoid robots
- Master perception and VSLAM with Isaac ROS
- Learn navigation and path planning with Nav2
- Understand Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems for natural humanoid interaction
- Master voice-to-action with speech models
- Learn cognitive planning with LLMs and ROS 2
- Build complete autonomous humanoid systems
